## Register

product

## Users
- **Moroccan Radiology Technologists**: Candidates looking for active recruitment notices, tracking their exam dates, deadlines, locations, and radiology-specific seat allocations.
- **Active Job Seekers**: Candidates who need to track their application status through a complex, multi-stage government recruitment timeline (Notice -> List of Candidates -> Exam Planning -> Results -> Definitive Assignments).
- **System Administrators**: Administrators who need to quickly verify parsed data, update decisions, edit notes, and verify candidate matches.

## Product Purpose
CNCR Watcher is a hosted tracker for recruitment notices from the Moroccan Ministry of Health, focusing on radiology opportunities. It replaces cluttered, slow government portals with a reliable, structured, and rapid overview of all relevant opportunities, complete with Gemini-powered automated parsing and name matching.

## Brand Personality
- **Warm & Trustworthy**: Calm cream and sand tones, elegant yet clear typography, soft shadows.
- **Extremely Polished**: Clean borders, precise layout consistency, and high-fidelity spacing inspired by Stripe Dashboard and Tailwind UI.
- **No-Nonsense Utility**: Clean hierarchy that prioritizes instant legibility and data density without visual noise.

## Anti-references
- **Moroccan Government Clutter**: Broken alignments, heavy text, chaotic tables, and generic styling.
- **SaaS/AI Slop**: Unnecessary gradient text, excessive emojis, useless cards, and glassmorphic blurs.
- **Loud Technical Accents**: Cold, high-contrast dark modes or neon colors that look like a gaming dashboard.
- **Corporate Spreadsheet Boring**: Lifeless, cold-gray spreadsheets with zero delight or character.

## Design Principles
1. **Calm Clarity over Clutter**: Serving as an oasis of reassurance. Warm, soothing neutral tones (cream, linen, sand) and generous, structured spacing to keep users calm while tracking high-stress job opportunities.
2. **Speed & Accessibility at the Doorstep**: Optimizing for candidates checking results on mobile devices outside exam halls in direct sunlight, requiring absolute legibility, high-contrast statuses, and rapid loading.
3. **Bulletproof Timeline Hierarchy**: Clearly representing the lifecycle of a recruitment notice (Notice, List, Planning, Results, Assignments) in a unified, digestible chronological unit.
4. **Refined Craftsmanship**: Emulating Stripe-level detail with perfect borders, subtle interactive states, and consistent, robust button/control styling.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **WCAG AA Compliance**: High-contrast text readability (tested under outdoor light conditions).
- **Arabic/French Bilingualism**: Graceful layouts that can accommodate both left-to-right (French) and right-to-left (Arabic names/titles) text sequences without breaking.
- **Extreme Mobile Responsiveness**: Perfect legibility on screens down to 320px width.
